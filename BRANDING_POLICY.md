## Fork Naming Rule

Forks and modified versions should use a clearly distinguishable name.

Recommended wording:

- "Fork of US Fashion Persona Screener"
- "Based on US Fashion Persona Screener"

- "Official US Fashion Persona Screener"
- "US Fashion Persona Screener Pro"
- "usfashionpersona.com official mirror"

unless written permission is granted by the owner.
